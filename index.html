About us

Acumen Global Sourcing Solutions (AGSS) is a one-stop international import company offering a professional and reliable service.

We are your one-stop international import company.

We are a fully registered on-the-ground British and Chinese company with expertise in product sourcing, purchasing, quality control, shipping and logistics. Our actions are valid under British, European and Chinese law, which is a major component when operating globally.
